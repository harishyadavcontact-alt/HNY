### 🎲 2. Luck & Unfairness in Extremistan

- Effort doesn’t equal outcome.

- Skill keeps you in the game; **luck wins it**.

- You can do everything “right” and still lose.

---

### 💡 3. Lessons from the Distributions

- **Most of life** behaves like Mediocristan.

- **Most of what matters** (wealth, war, collapse, love) lives in Extremistan.

- Wisdom = Treat the world as **fat-tailed by default**.

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|Domain|Traits|Examples|
|**Mediocristan**|Thin tails, small deviations, safe averages|Height, weight, IQ|
|**Extremistan**|Fat tails, large deviations, outlier-driven|Markets, war, fame, startups|

> ☠️ Mistaking one for the other is where systems fail.
