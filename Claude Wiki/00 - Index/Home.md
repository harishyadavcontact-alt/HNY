## Core Argument (One Sentence)

> Humans are exquisitely well-designed for an environment we no longer live in — and understanding evolution is the only toolkit powerful enough to solve that problem.

## The Spine

```
Selection (pre-biotic)
  + Heredity
  = Adaptation
    → Evolution
      → The Human Niche (paradox: jack of all trades, master of all)
        → Hyper-Novelty (pace of change > pace of adaptation)
          → Cryptic Dark Age
            → Need for evolutionary framework
```
