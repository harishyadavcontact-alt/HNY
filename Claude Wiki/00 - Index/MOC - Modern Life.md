# MOC — Modern Life Domains

> Each chapter of the book applies the evolutionary framework to a specific domain of modern life. The pattern is always the same: what we evolved for vs what we actually do now.

---

## The Template (Apply to Every Domain)

```
1. What did our ancestors do?  (EEA baseline)
2. What do we do now?  (modern behavior)
3. What is the gap?  (mismatch)
4. What are the costs of the gap?  (pathology, dysfunction)
5. What does evolution suggest?  (prescription)
```

## Key Insight

> Our bodies and brains are running software written for the Pleistocene. The modern world keeps installing new environments — but the hardware hasn't changed.
