# MOC — Sentient Life

> The traits we think are uniquely human have evolved independently in multiple lineages. Sentience is a convergent solution to a common problem.

---

## Properties of Sentient Life (Universal)

```
All sentient life — everywhere in the universe — will share:
  1. Long-lived (relative to other life on their planet)
  2. Social (permanent aggregations, not just safety-in-numbers)
  3. Long childhood → Plasticity
  4. Cultural transfer of information
  5. High encephalization (large brain relative to body)
```

## Key Insight

> When you find the same complex trait — theory of mind, tool use, cultural learning — evolving independently in corvids, cetaceans, and primates, you are not looking at accident. You are looking at a convergent solution to the demands of complex social life.
