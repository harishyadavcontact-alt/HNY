# MOC — The Human Niche

> The central paradox: we are jacks of all trades and masters of all of them. No other species has done this.

---

## The Paradox

```
Rule: Jack-of-all-trades = master of none
  → Specialization enables dominance of a niche
  → Breadth and mastery are in trade-off

Exception: Homo sapiens
  → Jack of nearly every trade
  → Master of nearly every habitat
  → Niche = unbounded

∴ The Human Paradox = a treasure map. Dig here.
```
