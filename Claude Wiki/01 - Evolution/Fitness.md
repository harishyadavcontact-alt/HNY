# Fitness

> What fitness is actually about: lodging your genes as far into the future as you can get them.

---

## The Real Definition

Fitness is not about being strong, healthy, or "fit" in the gym sense. It is about **reproductive success across generations** — more precisely, about how far your genetic lineage propagates into the future.

> "The good news is you are exquisitely well-designed. What fitness is about is about lodging your genes as far into the future as you can get them. The bad news is that you are exquisitely well-designed for an environment that we don't live in."
> — Bret Weinstein, Sophia Lectures

---

## The Measurement Problem

Biologists developed [[Reproductive Success]] as a proxy for fitness. It works ~95% of the time. The tragedy: when you get really good results with a proxy, you start to think the proxy *is* the thing.

### Where Reproductive Success Fails

- Sterile worker ants: reproduce zero times personally, yet carry the queen's genes forward — maximally fit through [[Lineage vs Individual|lineage fitness]]
- Organisms that trade current reproduction for future reproduction
- Any situation where indirect genetic propagation outperforms direct reproduction

---

## Fitness as Productive Tautology

"Survival of the fittest" (not Darwin's phrase, but useful) is close to a [[Tautology — Productive vs Useless|tautology]]: "the fittest survive" ≈ "those that survive are those that survive."

But it's a *productive* tautology — it orients your attention to the styles and strategies of competition even when you can't specify in advance exactly what the competition is about.
