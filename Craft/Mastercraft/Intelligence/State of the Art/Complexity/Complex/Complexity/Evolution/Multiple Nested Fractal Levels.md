# Multiple Nested Fractal Levels

> Lineages are fractal. You are simultaneously a lineage of one, a lineage of siblings, a lineage of cousins, a national lineage, a human lineage, a mammalian lineage. Selection operates at all of these levels simultaneously — prioritizing different levels at different times.

---

## The Fractal Nature of Lineage

A **[[Craft/Mastercraft/Intelligence/State of the Art/Complexity/Complex/Complexity/Evolution/Lineage]]** is an individual and all of that individual's descendants. This definition scales fractally:

- **You alone**: if you have no children, you are a lineage of one
- **Your nuclear family**: you + your children + their children = a lineage
- **Your extended family**: your most recent common ancestor + all descendants = a lineage
- **Your community / nation**: the most recent common ancestor of the group + all descendants
- **All humans**: the most recent common ancestor of Homo sapiens + all descendants
- **All primates, all mammals, all life**: each constitutes a nested lineage

> "You are simultaneously a lineage. You are part of the lineage represented by this room. You are part of the entire human lineage. You are a descendant of the most recent common ancestor of all primates, of all mammals, of all creatures."
> — Bret Weinstein, Sophia Lecture 2

---

## Why This Matters for Selection

Selection must decide which level of lineage to prioritize at any moment. This is not a conscious decision — it is the outcome of adaptive pressures across evolutionary time.

- **Sometimes it's about you and your kids**: individual reproductive success is maximized
- **Sometimes it's about your kin group**: kin selection, inclusive fitness
- **Sometimes it's about your nation or culture**: lineage-level investment in institutions, monuments, wars
- **Sometimes it's about the species**: species-level adaptations that constrain individual variation

The tragedy of the fitness proxy problem is that biologists settled on individual reproductive success as a measure of fitness — which works 95% of the time but fails catastrophically in exactly the cases that matter most: [[Craft/Mastercraft/Intelligence/State of the Art/Complexity/Complex/Complexity/Evolution/eusociality-|eusociality]], [[Notre Dame Paradox|monument-building]], human culture.

---

## The Illusion of Self

> "We are programmed to be focused on ourselves because evolution occurred at a moment where we didn't have a lot of reach. So our focus on ourselves is really about the limit of our tools."
> — Bret Weinstein

The self feels like the natural unit of selection. But this is an artifact of the scale at which ancestral humans could act. Selection doesn't care about you particularly — it cares about what level of lineage your actions serve.

Three generations from now, the number of your specific genes in the gene pool depends mostly on the success of the broader gene pool in which your genes circulate — not on anything you can do individually.

---

## Practical Implications

Understanding nested fractal levels helps explain:

- **Religion and cathedral-building**: investment in civilizational lineage, not individual lineage
- **Soldiers dying without offspring**: investment in national/cultural lineage
- **Celibate clergy**: lineage investment that bypasses individual reproduction
- **Cultural norms and institutions**: tools for managing lineage fitness at a scale beyond the individual
