Something is **unscalable** when it **hits constraints, diminishing returns, or breaks down** when you try to expand it.

**Unscalable examples:**

- **Artisanal crafts:** A master craftsman can't 10x output without compromising quality

- **Personal relationships:** You can't maintain 10,000 deep friendships

- **Local food systems:** A farmer's market model doesn't scale to feeding millions

- **Bespoke services:** Highly customized consulting doesn't scale like software

**Key characteristic:** Returns hit ceilings or diminish (linear or bounded upside).
