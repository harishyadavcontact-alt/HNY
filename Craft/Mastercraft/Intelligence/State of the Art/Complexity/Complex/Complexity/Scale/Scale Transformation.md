Scale transformation is the act of **changing the level of observation** - moving between different magnitudes, scopes, or timeframes.

**Types of scale transformation:**

- **Spatial:** Individual → Group → Population → System (one person to entire society)

- **Temporal:** Seconds → Hours → Years → Decades (short-term to long-term)

- **Magnitude:** Small dose → Medium dose → Large dose (quantity scaling)

- **Hierarchical:** Micro → Meso → Macro (organizational levels)
