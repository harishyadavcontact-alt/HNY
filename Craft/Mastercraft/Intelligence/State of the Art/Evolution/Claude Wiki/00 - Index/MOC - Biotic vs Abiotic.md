# MOC — Biotic vs Abiotic

> The universe had a proto-competitive logic before life existed. Life did not invent competition — it inherited and amplified it.

## The Transition

```
ABIOTIC UNIVERSE
  → 4 properties determine pattern prevalence:
      1. Tendency to come into being
      2. Durability once formed
      3. Similarity (recognizability as a category)
      4. Tendency to accumulate limited stuff  ← proto-competition

  + HEREDITY (information passed across generations)

= BIOTIC UNIVERSE
  → Selection becomes true competition
  → Striving (organisms pursue objectives they don't understand)
  → Adaptation (accumulated traits)
```

## Key Insight

> A salmon is mostly water. Water flows downhill. The salmon flows uphill. The question is not *how* — it is *why*. The answer is: heredity added to selection creates organisms that strive toward objectives they do not understand.
