# MOC — Hyper-Novelty

> Our environment is so radically different from the one we evolved in, and changing so fast, that it is not possible even in principle for us to keep up.

---

## The Problem

```
We are designed for Environment A (hunter-gatherer, small tribe, known terrain)
We live in Environment B (postindustrial, anonymous masses, infinite novelty)

Rate of environmental change > Rate of biological adaptation

∴ We are perpetually a fish out of water
∴ We are perpetually a bee trapped in a house
```
