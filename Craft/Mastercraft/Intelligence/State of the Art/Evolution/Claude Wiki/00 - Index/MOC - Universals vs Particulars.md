# MOC — Universals vs Particulars

> The math inside the nautilus shell is universal. The shell itself is a particular. This distinction is one of the most powerful in all of science.

---

## The Framework

```
UNIVERSAL
  → Laws of physics, mathematics
  → Fibonacci series (1,1,2,3,5,8,13...)
  → Gold (atomic number 79 — will be the same everywhere)
  → Properties of sentient life
  → Will be discovered/encountered by all sentient life everywhere

PARTICULAR
  → This specific nautilus shell on this planet
  → What humans have done with gold
  → Spiral staircase at Sagrada Familia
  → The instantiation of a universal in a specific context
```
