# Lineage vs Individual

> The unit of selection that matters is not the individual — it is the lineage.

---

## Why This Matters

Evolutionary biology made a measurement error: it tracked individual reproductive success as the proxy for fitness. This leads to paradoxes.

**The ant problem:** A sterile worker ant reproduces zero times. By individual reproductive success metrics, it is the least fit organism in the colony. Yet by lineage fitness, it is doing exactly what evolution designed it to do — the queen carries their shared genes forward.

**The real unit:** The lineage. The propagation of a genetic line through time. Individual organisms are temporary vehicles.

---

## Implications

1. **Kin selection** becomes obvious — helping relatives propagate your shared genes *is* fitness
2. **Eusociality** (ant colonies, bee hives) is not a paradox once you shift to lineage as the unit
3. **Altruism** toward genetic relatives is not selfless — it is lineage-level self-interest
