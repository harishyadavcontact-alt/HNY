1. **Awareness (TOFU)**: Broadest part of the funnel—many prospects enter here.

1. **Interest (MOFU)**: Fewer prospects remain, showing interest in learning more.

1. **Consideration (MOFU)**: Further filtering as prospects seriously evaluate your solution.

1. **Intent (BOFU)**: Prospects are ready to make a decision but need the final push.

1. **Purchase (BOFU)**: The funnel narrows down to those who convert into paying customers.

1. **Post-Purchase/Retention**: Beyond the funnel, focusing on customer loyalty and advocacy.
