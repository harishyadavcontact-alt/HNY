# Stick

- Forced Rituals and Initiations:
    
    Cults use rituals and initiations to instill loyalty, obedience, and a sense of belonging.
    

- Punishments
    
    Creation of secret police or paramilitary organizations to enforce control.
    

- Sleep DeprivationSome cults restrict sleep to weaken members’ resistance and foster dependency on the group.

- Forced ConfessionsCult leaders may pressure or force members to confess personal information or past sins, which can later be used as leverage against them.

- Sacrifice: Genocide
    
    - This is the deliberate killing of a large group of people, often for political or religious reasons.
    

- Threats of Harm:
    
    Some cults may use threats of harm to members or their families to prevent them from leaving or speaking out against the group.
    

- Guilt and shame Tripping
    
    Con men may guilt-trip their victims into complying with their demands or handing over money.
    
    Manipulating the individual to believe they are inherently flawed or sinful, leading to compliance for redemption.
    

- Embarrassment
    
    - * This is the act of making someone feel ashamed or humiliated in order to control them. This can be done through public humiliation, insults, or threats.* **
