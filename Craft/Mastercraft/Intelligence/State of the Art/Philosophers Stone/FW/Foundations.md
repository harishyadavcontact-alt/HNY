**2. Focus on Asymmetry**

- “Exposure is more important than knowledge; decision effects supersede logic”.

**3. The Triad Heuristic**

All things can be classified into three categories based on their exposure to disorder:

- **FRAGILE:** Harmed by volatility; wants tranquility. (E.g., centralized nation-state)

- **ROBUST:** Neither harmed nor helped by volatility and disorder

- **ANTIFRAGILE:** Benefits from volatility and disorder; “grows from disorder”. (E.g., decentralized systems; tinkering)
