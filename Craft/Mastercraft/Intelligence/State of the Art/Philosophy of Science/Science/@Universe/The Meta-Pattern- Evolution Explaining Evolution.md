### Self-Referential Coherence

We are products of evolution using evolved cognitive tools to understand the process that produced us.

- Our epistemology is evolutionary: We recognize patterns because pattern-recognizers out-survived pattern-blind organisms.

- Our aesthetics are evolutionary: We find beauty in symmetry, fractals, golden ratios because those patterns predicted fitness-relevant realities in ancestral environments.

- Our morality is evolutionary: Cooperation, reciprocity, fairness intuitions emerge from lineage-level selection.

The toolkit works not despite being evolved, but because it's evolved—it's been tested against reality for millions of years.
