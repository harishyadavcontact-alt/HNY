### Seven Independent Evolutions of Proto-Sentience

- Primates (us, baboons—shared ancestor gave us these traits)

- Corvids (crows, ravens—tool use, theory of mind precursors)

- Parrots (macaws, cockatoos—vocal learning, social complexity)

- Toothed whales (dolphins, orcas—synchrony, cultural transmission)

- Elephants (grief, long memory, social networks)

- Wolves/Canids (cooperative hunting, play, hierarchy)

- Lions (unique among cats for sociality)

Shared preconditions:

- Long-lived (compared to other life on planet)

- Social (permanent groups, not just aggregations)

- Generational overlap (information transfer across 3+ generations)

- Long childhoods (time for learning, play, plasticity)
