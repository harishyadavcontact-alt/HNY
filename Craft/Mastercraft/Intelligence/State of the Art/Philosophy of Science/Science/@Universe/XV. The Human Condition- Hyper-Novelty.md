### Exquisitely Well-Designed...For the Wrong Environment

- **The good news:** You are optimized by millions of years of selection

- **The bad news:** You're optimized for an environment that no longer exists

**Hyper-novelty:** Our environment changes faster than biology can track—we're a bee trapped in a house, banging against windows that look like clear paths but aren't.

Cultural evolution's rate now exceeds genetic evolution's capacity to respond. Most modern cultural traits haven't stood the test of time yet—we don't know if video games are adaptive or maladaptive.
