**Agreement/Convergence of independent evidence streams**

"You can't have consilience between different kinds of evidence if you only have one kind of evidence."

**The Consilience Principle:**

- Multiple types of evidence must independently converge on same conclusion

- Single data type = pseudo-science, no matter how abundant

- **The antidote to reductionism**

**Why consilience matters:**

- "That is so often not actually going to result in a correct answer" (single-method approaches)

- Robust scientific understanding requires drawing from multiple clues

- Independent lines must agree for confidence
