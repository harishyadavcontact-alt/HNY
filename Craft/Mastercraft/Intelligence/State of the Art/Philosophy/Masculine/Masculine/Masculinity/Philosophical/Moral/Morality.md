Strength & honor

1. Strength without honor is evil

2. Honor without strength is impotence

3. Strength and honor is mastermorality

4. Perhaps risk is the determinant.  
    Virtue requires consistency, and honor requires courage.  
    

5. Consistency tests self mastery.  
    Courage tests character in the face of risk.  
    

6. Ergo, one could be virtuous but not necessarily courageous—it depends on actions made in face of risk.
