- Law of Identity
    
    - Symbolically: A = A.
    
    - If proposition p is true then p is true.
    

- Law of non Contradiction:
    
    - Things cannot be A & -A at the same time
    
    - It cannot be raining & non raining at the same time
    

- Law of Excluded Middle: OR
    
    - Any proposition either is true or its negation true- no third option
    
    - True of False
