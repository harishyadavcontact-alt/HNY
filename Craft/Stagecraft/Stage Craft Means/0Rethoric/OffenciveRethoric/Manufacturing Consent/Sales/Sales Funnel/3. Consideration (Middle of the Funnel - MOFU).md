In this stage, prospects are considering your product or service as a potential solution. They may be comparing prices, features, and reviews, and deciding whether your solution fits their needs and budget.

**Key goal**: Build trust, address objections, and prove your product’s value.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Case studies**: Showcasing how your product has successfully helped other customers.
    
    - **Product trials or demos**: Offering free trials or live demonstrations to allow prospects to experience the product.
    
    - **Detailed product information**: Providing guides, white papers, and FAQs that answer specific questions and concerns.
    
    - **Customer testimonials and reviews**: Using social proof to show the positive experiences of others.
