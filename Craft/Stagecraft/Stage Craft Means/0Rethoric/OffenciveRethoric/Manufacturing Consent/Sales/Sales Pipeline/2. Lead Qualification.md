- **What it is**: Not every prospect is a good fit, so this stage involves assessing which leads are worth pursuing. The sales team qualifies leads based on factors such as their need for the product, budget, authority to make decisions, and readiness to buy.

- **Activities**: Engaging with prospects, asking qualifying questions, and using lead scoring techniques to assess the likelihood of conversion.

**Key Metric**: Number of qualified leads.

### 2. Lead Qualification

- **Objective**: Evaluate leads to determine whether they are worth pursuing.

- **Process**:
    
    - **BANT**: Use qualification frameworks like BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, and Timing) to assess lead viability.
    
    - **Scoring**: Assign scores to leads based on their engagement and profile to prioritize follow-up actions.
    
    - **Engage**: Initial contact with the lead to understand their needs and fit with your product or service.
