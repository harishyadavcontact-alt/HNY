Sales metrics help teams analyze performance, identify areas for improvement, and refine strategies. Key metrics include:

- **Conversion rate**: The percentage of leads that convert into sales.

- **Average deal size**: The typical value of a sale.

- **Sales cycle length**: How long it takes to close a deal.

- **Customer acquisition cost (CAC)**: The cost of acquiring a new customer.

- **Customer lifetime value (CLTV)**: The total revenue a customer will bring over their lifetime.
