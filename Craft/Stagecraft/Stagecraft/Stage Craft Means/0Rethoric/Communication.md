- **Intentionality**
    
    - **“Know your intention clearly and precisely.”**
    
    - This is the _engine_—without it, the rest collapses.
    
    - You must know what you want to _signal_, not just say.
    
    - Do you want to amuse? To seduce? To negotiate? To provoke? To bond? To test?
    
    **Intentionality** makes communication _functional_—you calibrate your signals not for truth but for _effect_. And because people infer meaning, not receive it, you must _plant it_ in their cognitive soil.
    
    > “The difference between a threat and a joke is intent.” — Talebian principle of asymmetry
    

- **Implicit**
    
    **Speak in Statements, Not Questions**
    
    - Questions can be **defensive**, **weak**, or **overly literal**.
    
    - Statements show **conviction**, and even when they're ambiguous, they push forward.
    
    Compare:
    
    > “Do you like me?”
    > 
    > vs
    > 
    > “You like me.”
    
    The first begs. The second _frames_.
    
    This is the **rhetoric of framing**. Good communication frames reality by asserting it indirectly.
    
    ## **Heuristic 5: Implicit > Explicit**
    
    - Explicit communication is **overfitted** to context.
    
    - Implicit communication is **robust** to multiple contexts—and allows **receiver-dependent meaning**.
    

- **Indirect**
    
    - Indirection preserves **optional meaning**, **ambiguity**, and **asymmetry**.
    
    - It allows your words to carry **convexity**: they scale differently depending on the listener.
    
    - The powerful never speak literally—they imply. _(“Nice house.” = “I could take it.”)_
    
    **Direct speech collapses interpretive space. Indirect speech** _**opens**_ **it.**
    

- **Informal**
    
    - Formality is a **defensive protocol**—it is used when risk must be reduced, not when creativity is required.
    
    - Informality allows for _emergence_, _play_, _volatility_.
    
    - Jokes, flirtation, poetic imagery—these need **room to collapse and reassemble**.
    
    Formality is **fragile**: it shatters under improvisation. Informality is **antifragile**: it bends, adapts, and multiplies under stress.
