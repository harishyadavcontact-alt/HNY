- This model further explains how society is organized. The _base_ (the economic structure) consists of the means of production and the relations of production (i.e., how labor is organized). The _superstructure_ (culture, politics, ideology) arises from and legitimizes the base. The base shapes the superstructure, but the superstructure also reinforces the base. For example, legal systems and governments exist to protect property rights and capitalism, while culture and ideology promote capitalist values.
