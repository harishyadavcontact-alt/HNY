At this stage, prospects are aware of their problem and are actively searching for solutions. They may be comparing your product with competitors and doing research. This is where they start showing more interest in how your product can solve their needs.

**Key goal**: Educate prospects and nurture their interest in your solution.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Lead magnets**: Offering free eBooks, webinars, or other valuable resources in exchange for contact information.
    
    - **Email marketing**: Sending educational content, case studies, and product information to nurture leads.
    
    - **Retargeting ads**: Showing ads to people who have previously visited your website or engaged with your content.
    
    - **Product demos**: Offering live or pre-recorded demonstrations that highlight how your product works.
