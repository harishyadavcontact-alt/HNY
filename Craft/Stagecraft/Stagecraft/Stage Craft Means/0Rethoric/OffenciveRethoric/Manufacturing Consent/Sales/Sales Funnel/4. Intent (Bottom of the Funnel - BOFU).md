At this point, the prospect has shown clear intent to purchase. They have most likely expressed interest through an action such as requesting a quote, signing up for a trial, or adding products to their cart.

**Key goal**: Encourage a purchasing decision and close the deal.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Personalized offers**: Offering discounts, limited-time promotions, or special deals to incentivize a purchase.
    
    - **Sales calls**: Direct communication via phone or email to address any final objections and push for the sale.
    
    - **Product comparisons**: Offering easy-to-understand comparisons between your product and competitors to show why yours is the best option.
    
    - **Customer support**: Providing quick, efficient answers to any last-minute questions or concerns.
