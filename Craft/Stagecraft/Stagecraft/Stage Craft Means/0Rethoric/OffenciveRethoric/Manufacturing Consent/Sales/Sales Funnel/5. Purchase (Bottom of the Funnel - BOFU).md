This is the final stage where the prospect makes the decision to buy your product or service. The key here is to make the transaction as smooth as possible and ensure that the customer has a good experience, encouraging them to return and potentially recommend your product to others.

**Key goal**: Facilitate the purchase and ensure customer satisfaction.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Seamless checkout process**: Ensure the purchasing process is quick, easy, and hassle-free.
    
    - **Post-purchase follow-up**: Send confirmation emails, thank you notes, and provide access to customer support.
    
    - **Upselling and cross-selling**: Suggest related products or services to increase the value of the sale.
