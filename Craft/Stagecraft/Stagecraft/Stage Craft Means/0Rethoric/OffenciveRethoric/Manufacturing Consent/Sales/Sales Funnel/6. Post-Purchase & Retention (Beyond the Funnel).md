After the purchase, your goal shifts to keeping the customer satisfied, encouraging repeat business, and generating referrals. Post-purchase engagement builds customer loyalty and maximizes the **customer lifetime value (CLV)**.

**Key goal**: Retain customers and encourage repeat purchases.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Customer support**: Provide exceptional post-sale support to resolve any issues and ensure customer satisfaction.
    
    - **Loyalty programs**: Offer rewards for repeat purchases, referrals, or ongoing subscriptions.
    
    - **Surveys and feedback**: Collect customer feedback to improve your product and services.
    
    - **Upselling and cross-selling**: Offer additional or upgraded products based on the customer’s purchase history.
