- **What it is**: This is the process of identifying and researching potential customers (leads). During this stage, the sales team looks for individuals or businesses that fit the ideal customer profile (ICP) and could benefit from the product or service.

- **Activities**: Cold calling, email outreach, networking, social media research, attending events or conferences.

**Key Metric**: Number of new leads generated.

### 3. Prospecting

- **Objective**: Build relationships with leads to move them into the sales funnel.

- **Process**:
    
    - **Introductory Meeting**: Schedule calls or meetings to introduce your solution.
    
    - **Discovery**: Ask questions to uncover the lead’s pain points, challenges, and goals.
    
    - **Value Proposition**: Present how your product or service can solve their specific problems.
