- **What it is**: Once the needs are clearly identified, the salesperson presents a customized proposal or offer to the prospect. This could include pricing, product specifications, and terms of service.

- **Activities**: Preparing proposals, sending pricing quotes, and drafting contracts.

### 5. Proposal/Quote

- **Objective**: Present a formal solution that aligns with the lead’s needs.

- **Process**:
    
    - **Customization**: Tailor the proposal to meet specific needs uncovered during the discovery.
    
    - **Pricing**: Provide transparent and competitive pricing.
    
    - **Timeline**: Offer realistic timelines for delivery or implementation.
