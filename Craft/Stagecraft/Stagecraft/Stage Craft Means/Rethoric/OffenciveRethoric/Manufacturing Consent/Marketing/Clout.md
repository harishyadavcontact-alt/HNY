# Clout

# The Room

- Judgement
    
    - Court attention at all costs
    
    - Burn brightly than those around you
    
    - Attracting attention is a skill that can be learned
    
    - Attach your name and reputation to an image that sets you apart
    
    - Once the image is established, you have a place in the sky for your star
    
    - Everything is judged by its appearance; what is unseen counts for nothing.
    
    - Never let yourself get lost in the crowd, then, or buried in oblivion. Stand out.
    
    Everything is judged by its appearance; what is unseen counts for nothing. Never let yourself get lost in the crowd, then, or buried in oblivion. Stand out. Be conspicuous, at all cost. Make yourself a magnet of attention by appearing larger, more colorful, more mysterious than the bland and timid masses.
