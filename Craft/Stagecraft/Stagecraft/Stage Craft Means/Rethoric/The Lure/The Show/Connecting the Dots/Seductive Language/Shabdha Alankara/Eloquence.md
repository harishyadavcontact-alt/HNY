# Eloquence

- The power of words
    
    **Words are the quickest way to create emotional disturbance.** They can **uplift**, **elevate**, **stir anger**, without referring to anything real.
    

- The devices
    
    1. C**atchwords**
    
    1. S**logans**
    
    1. R**hythmic repetitions**
    
    1. **Phrases**
    
    for the audience to repeat—can easily be acquired.
    

- Slow authoritative over passionate.
    
    1. The **slow, authoritative style** is often more effective than passion in the long run, for it is more subtly spellbinding, and less tiring.
    
    1. **Slow and hypnotic**
    
    1. I**magery**
    
    1. **alliteration**
    
    1. **Biblical rhetoric**
    

- Caught up, emotionally in your words
    
    **To bring off this kind of eloquence, it helps if the speaker is as emotional, as caught up in words, as the audience is.**
