- Just Cause
    
    - Defence against Aggression
    
    - Protection of innocents
    
    - Restoration of Justice(not conquest or gain)
    

- Legitimate Authority
    
    - Force may only be used by properly constituted public authority, The rules bears the waord as God’s Servant
    

- Right Intention
    
    - Aim must be peace & justice, not hatred or revenge
    

- Last Resort
    
    - All non-violent options must be exhausted
    

- Proportionality
    
    - Harm caused must not exceed the good achived
    

- Discrimination(Non-Comabatant Immunity)
    
    - Delibrate targeting of civilians - innocents is forbidden
    

- Reasonable Chance of Sucess
    
    - Avoid Futile Destruction
