### The Missing Layers (Convex Additions to the Barbell)

**1. Firearms & Defensive Capability (Offense in Chaos)**  
High-convexity tail hedge: In a grid-down / post-cycle world, the ability to **defend** what you've hedged becomes the dominant asymmetry.

- **Why necessary** — Faraday cages save your radio; they don't stop armed groups. History (every collapse, blackout, disaster) shows predation spikes when law dissolves.
- **Barbell posture** —
    - **Defense core (90%)**: Proficient, legal ownership of reliable platforms (e.g., semi-auto rifle + pistol + shotgun). Training > gear. Ammo stockpiles (thousands of rounds).
    - **Offense sliver (10%)**: Asymmetric tools (night vision, suppressors where legal, fortified positions).
- **Heuristic** — ""Gold buys nothing when men with guns decide otherwise."" Elites don't publicize arsenals, but reports confirm many (Zuckerberg compound rumors, Thiel-adjacent preppers) stockpile firearms + training. Military COG sites include armed security.
- **Via negativa** — Remove the fragility of being defenseless. If you can't enforce your hedge, it's concave.
