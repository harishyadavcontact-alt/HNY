- Descent with Modification in Practice
    
    Characters (not "characteristics"—biologists are intransigent) = the individual things that change over time.
    
    Example cascade (same structure, different instantiation):
    
    Lamprey gill arches → Shark jaws + gills → Mammal jaws + middle ear bones
    
    The first/second gill arch of an ancient vertebrate became your ability to chew and hear high frequencies.
