Rest assured, desire can still be kept in check by differences which do still exist in modernity. The example of class that I just highlighted is one of them. An even more trivial example is I’ve noticed that while sophomores in college may be more threatened if another sophomore gets a great internship, they aren’t so much when a junior gets an equally promising one. Because they reason, “Well, they got another year on me so they should be getting something better.” Even such minute differences is enough to create social distance.
