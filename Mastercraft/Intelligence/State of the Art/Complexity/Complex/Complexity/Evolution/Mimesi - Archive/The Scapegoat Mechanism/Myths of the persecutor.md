Deceitfulness covered by unanimous mythology

And importantly, this deceitfulness is always occluded by myth because myth is written from the perspective of the persecutor. The persecutor writes from a position of the crowd, and from that vantage point, all will seem real – the blame, the praise, the deification, are all deserved and not mere projections.

But more importantly, none of this could be revealed – because if they were then gods would lose their powers if people realized that it was they themselves who through unanimity projected the power onto them. It’s all deceitful and all arbitrary.
