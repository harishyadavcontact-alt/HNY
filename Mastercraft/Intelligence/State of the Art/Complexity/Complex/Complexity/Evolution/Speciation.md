The Branching process of evolution.

vs. Species: focues on process not charasteric

- The Process Over the Category
    
    Speciation (the branching event) is real. Species (the category) is contested.
    
    Why? Biology doesn't build discrete categories for human convenience. We have dozens of species concepts depending on context:
    
    - Biological (can they interbreed?)
    
    - Paleontological (fossils can't breed—need morphology)
    
    - Asexual organisms (clones?)
