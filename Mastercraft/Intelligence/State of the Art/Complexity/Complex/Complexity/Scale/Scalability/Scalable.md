Something is **scalable** when it can be **increased or expanded without fundamentally breaking or requiring restructuring**.

**Positive scalability examples:**

- **Digital products:** Software scales from 10 users to 10 million users with minimal marginal cost

- **Leverage:** A good idea in finance scales to billions without losing effectiveness

- **Networks:** Platform effects - value increases superlinearly with users (Metcalfe's law)

- **Media:** A book, song, or film can reach unlimited audiences

**Key characteristic:** Returns scale linearly or superlinearly (power law upside).
