Non-Linearity
        
        - **Super-Linearity**
            
            - Output increases **more than proportionally** with input.
            
            - Common in **innovation, networks, cities, virality**.
            
            - Example: In cities, a 10x population increase leads to **17x GDP**, not 10x.
            
        
        - **Sub-Linearity**
            
            - Output increases **less than proportionally** with input.
            
            - Seen in **resource scaling**, economies of scale, some biological systems.
            
            - Example: A 10x increase in animal mass leads to only ~6x increase in metabolic rate.
