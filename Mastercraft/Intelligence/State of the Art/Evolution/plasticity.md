### Why Long Childhoods Evolve

- **Zygotic inheritance:** All instructions at birth (most organisms—frogs, insects)

- **Plasticity:** Instructions require experience to complete (mammals, birds, humans)

Human neural development:

- Massive overproduction of neurons and connections in early childhood

- Selective elimination of 70% of connections by adulthood

- You keep what works given your environment

This is exploration, not waste. Overproduction → test against reality → prune = adaptive flexibility.
