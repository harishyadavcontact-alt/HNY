    - Charismatic Leader: Blind Loyalty
        
        Cultivate a charismatic leader’s personality to foster blind loyalty and devotion.
        
        Cults are usually centered around a charismatic leader who claims to have special knowledge, abilities, or a divine connection.
        
        The leader uses their charisma and persuasive skills to gain followers and maintain control over them.
        
        Hitler presented himself as a charismatic leader and cultivated a cult of personality around him. He was able to inspire fervent loyalty and devotion among his followers.
        
        Foster devotion and loyalty to a charismatic leader by promoting their infallibility or divine status.
