# Vulnerable Targest

- Victim Theory: Exploitation of Vulnerabilities
    
    Cults often target individuals who are vulnerable or going through difficult times in their lives.
    
    They may offer a sense of belonging, purpose, or identity to those who are searching for answers or experiencing feelings of loneliness, dissatisfaction, or loss.
    
    This involves targeting people who are more likely to be susceptible to manipulation, such as children, the elderly, or people with disabilities.
    
    For example, a political campaign might use this strategy by targeting children with a campaign ad that plays on their fears or emotions.
    

- Understand the electorate
    
    The first step is to understand the electorate. This includes understanding their demographics, their values, and their concerns. You need to tailor your message and your campaign strategy to appeal to the voters who are most likely to support you.
    

- Voters over haters
    
    Voters is what you need the rest, use them to make your victory assured.
