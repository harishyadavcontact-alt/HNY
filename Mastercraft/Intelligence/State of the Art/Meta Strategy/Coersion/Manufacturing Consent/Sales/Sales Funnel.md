### 4. **Sales Funnel**

- **Objective**: Guide leads through the sales journey toward conversion.

- **Process**:
    
    - **Awareness**: The lead becomes aware of your solution.
    
    - **Interest**: The lead shows interest by engaging with your content or booking meetings.
    
    - **Consideration**: The lead evaluates your solution in comparison to competitors.
    
    - **Intent**: The lead signals a strong interest and might request proposals or trials.
    
    - **Decision**: The lead makes a purchasing decision.
