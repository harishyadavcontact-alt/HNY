This is the first stage, where potential customers become aware of your product, service, or brand. They may come across your business through various channels, such as:

- Social media

- Paid advertising (PPC, social ads)

- Blog posts or SEO

- Word of mouth

- Email marketing

- Trade shows or events

At this stage, the prospect may not know much about your brand or product but has identified a problem or need.

**Key goal**: Make people aware of your business and capture their interest.

- Strategies:
    
    - **Content marketing**: Blog posts, videos, and infographics that answer common questions or address pain points.
    
    - **Paid ads**: Advertisements on search engines or social media targeting relevant keywords or demographics.
    
    - **SEO**: Optimize your website and content for search engines so that potential customers find you when they search for related topics.
