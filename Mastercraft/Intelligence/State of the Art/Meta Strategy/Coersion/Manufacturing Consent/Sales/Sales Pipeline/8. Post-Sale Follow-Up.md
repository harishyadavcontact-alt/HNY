- **What it is**: After the sale is closed, it’s important to follow up with the customer to ensure satisfaction, offer support, and look for upsell or cross-sell opportunities. This also helps in maintaining long-term relationships for future sales.

- **Activities**: Sending thank you notes, onboarding, providing customer support, and checking in on the customer’s experience.

**Key Metric**: Number of follow-up actions taken, customer satisfaction rate.
