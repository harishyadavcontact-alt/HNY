

A fantasy refers to a mental or imaginative scenario or scenario that a person may find sexually arousing or exciting. These fantasies can involve a wide range of scenarios, people, or situations and can be entirely fictional or based on real-life experiences. Fantasies are a normal and healthy aspect of human sexuality, and many people engage in sexual fantasies to enhance their sexual arousal and satisfaction. Fantasies can be shared with a partner for mutual enjoyment or kept private and personal.

Sexual fantasies serve various purposes, including escapism from sexual restraints, exploring different roles, and enhancing sexual pleasure. They can also be used to cope with stress or settle relational difficulties. Attachment theory suggests that sexual fantasies may fulfill emotional security needs. From an evolutionary perspective, gender differences in sexual fantasies may be related to reproductive strategies.
