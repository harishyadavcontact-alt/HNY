- Victim Theory:  Exploitation of Vulnerabilities
    
    Look at the people around you. Forget their social exterior, their obvi-ous character traits; look behind all of that, focusing on the gaps, the miss-ing pieces in their psyche. That is the raw material of any seduction.
    
    This chapter outlines the eighteen types of victims, each one of which has a dominant lack. Although your target may well reveal the qualities of more than one type, there is usually a common need that ties them to-gether.
    
    Never try to seduce someone who is of your own type. You will be like two puzzles missing the same parts.
