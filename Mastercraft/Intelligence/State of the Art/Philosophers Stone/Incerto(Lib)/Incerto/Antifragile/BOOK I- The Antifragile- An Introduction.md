**T**he first two chapters introduce and illustrate antifragility.

Chapter 3 introduces a distinction between the organic and the mechanical, say, between your cat and a washing machine.

Chapter 4 is about how the antifragility of some comes from the fragility of others, how errors benefit some, not others—the sort of things people tend to call evolution and write a lot, a lot about.
