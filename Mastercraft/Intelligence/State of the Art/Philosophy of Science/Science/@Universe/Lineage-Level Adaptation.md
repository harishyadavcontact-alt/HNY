The Cathedral Paradox

### Why Notre Dame Exists

If evolution is about individual reproductive success, how does a celibate bishop spend 100 years building a cathedral that takes 600 years to complete?

Answer: He's not optimizing for his genes. He's optimizing for lineages he belongs to:

- His religious lineage (Catholicism)

- His cultural lineage (French civilization)

- His species lineage (human meaning-making)

**Eusociality in humans: We're not eusocial like ants (haplodiploidy), but we exhibit lineage-level cooperation through culture.**
