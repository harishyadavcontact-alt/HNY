### The Mammalian Innovation

- **Sexual reproduction:** >500 million years old

- **Love:** Much newer—emerged with obligate maternal care

Lactation (mammary glands) created the first love: mother-infant bond. From that foundation:

- Pair bonding (monogamy in some lineages)

- Sibling/family love

- Friendship (non-kin reciprocal bonds)

- Abstract love (country, ideals)

Grief = the unavoidable cost of love. Elephants stand vigil over dead calves. Orca mothers carry dead infants for 17 days. Japanese Akita waits at train station for dead professor for 10 years.
