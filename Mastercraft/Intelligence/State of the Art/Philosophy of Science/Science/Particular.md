**Maxim:** Particulars are evolutionary products contingent on specific historical circumstances.
**Central Argument:**  
Particulars are not universal or invariant. They are evolutionary products. They are contingent on specific historical circumstances. Life on Earth is a particular. The shell is a particular. How humans use gold is particular. All particulars are contingent on having evolved under specific conditions that could have been different.

**Source:** [EXPLICIT] Lecture 1 (Heather Heying) - “The shell itself is a particular of life as it is evolved on this planet.”
 - “Even though the ways the things that we have done with gold will be particular but gold itself is a universal.”

**Epistemological Status:** Particulars are the historical products, not discovered universals. They are contingent on evolution and history.

**Contrast:** Gold is universal (the element with atomic number 79). How humans use gold is particular (cultural, historical, contingent).
