- social, economic and cultural life is black swan domain, physical life much less
    
    Social, economic, and cultural life lie in the Black Swan domain, physical life much less so.
    
    Further, the idea is to separate domains into those in which these Black Swans are both unpredictable and consequential, and
    
    those in which rare events are of no serious concern, either because they are predictable or because they are inconsequential.
    

  

- Success is now planetary or nothing, socio economic is in extreme extremistan
    
    Now, what is worse, because of modernity, the share of Extremistan is increasing. Winner-take-all effects are worsening: success for an author, a company, an idea, a musician, an athlete is planetary, or nothing. These worsen predictability since almost everything in socioeconomic life now is dominated by Black Swans. Our sophistication continuously puts us ahead of ourselves, creating things we are less and less capable of understanding.
