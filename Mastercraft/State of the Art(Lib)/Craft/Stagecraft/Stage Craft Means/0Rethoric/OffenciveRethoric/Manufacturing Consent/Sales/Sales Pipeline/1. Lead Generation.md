- **Objective**: Identify potential customers (leads).

- **Process**:
    
    - **Research**: Use market research, networking, referrals, or tools like LinkedIn, Google Ads, and social media to identify potential leads.
    
    - **Inbound**: Attract leads through content marketing, SEO, social media, or other marketing efforts.
    
    - **Outbound**: Actively reach out to potential leads through cold calls, emails, or advertising campaigns.
