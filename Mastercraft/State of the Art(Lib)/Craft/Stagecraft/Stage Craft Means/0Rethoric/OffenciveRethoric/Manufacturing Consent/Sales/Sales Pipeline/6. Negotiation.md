- **What it is**: During this stage, the prospect may raise concerns or objections related to the proposal. Negotiations occur around pricing, terms, features, or delivery. The sales team works to address these objections and finalize the deal.

- **Activities**: Handling objections, discussing terms, and adjusting proposals.

**Key Metric**: Number of deals in negotiation.

### 6. Negotiation

- **Objective**: Address any objections or concerns and reach a mutual agreement.

- **Process**:
    
    - **Overcoming Objections**: Clarify any issues related to cost, functionality, or timing.
    
    - **Concessions**: Offer discounts, additional services, or flexible terms if necessary.
    
    - **Agreement**: Finalize the terms, including the price, deliverables, and timelines.
