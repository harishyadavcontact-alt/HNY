# Fetistisitation

- Fetish
    
    A fetish is a strong, recurring sexual interest or attraction to a specific object, body part, material, or situation that is not typically considered sexual in nature. Fetishes can be very specific and unique to each individual. Common examples include foot fetishes (attraction to feet), leather fetishes (attraction to leather clothing or accessories), or latex fetishes (attraction to latex materials). People with fetishes often find these objects or situations sexually arousing or necessary for sexual satisfaction.
    
    When sexual arousal is achieved by or dependent on the use of objects, it is referred to as [sexual fetishism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sexual_fetishism), or in some instances a [paraphilia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paraphilia).

- Commodity Fetishism
    
    Is the process by which commodities appear to have inherent value and power beyond their use value (their utility) and exchange value (their market price). In a capitalist society, commodities are imbued with a sense of mystique and social significance that goes beyond their physical characteristics. This mystique can be seen as a form of fetishization, where people attribute almost magical qualities to commodities.
